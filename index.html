Rajnagar Gram Panchayat

Rajnagar, Daspur, Paschim Medinipur, 721211

Rajnagar Gram Panchayat of Daspur-I Panchayat Samiti of Paschim Medinipur Zilla Parishad is situated at Rajnagar, Chetua Rajnagar, Daspur, 721211. Rajangar GP area is 2,735 sqKM. In North side we have Ghatal Block separeted by Silaboti River. In south we have Sarberia-I GP separeted by Kangsaboti River. In East side we have Daspur-I GP and in West side we have Nij Narajole GP. Rajnagar GP has 19 Mouza and 22 Gram Sansad. According to 2011 census total population of GP is 28,612 out of which 14,757 Males and 13,855 Females. Total Family number is 7761 out of which SC Family is 2041, ST Family is 242, OBC Family is 582 and General Family is 4896. Total Family number which are Below Poverty Level (B.P.L) is 1710 out of which SC Family is 590 St Family is 138 OBC Family is 33 and General Family is 949.
